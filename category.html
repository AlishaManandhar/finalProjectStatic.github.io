One-Time Rentals

Lock in a show-stopping outfit for a special day and keep items for 4 or 8 days at a time.

Reserve Styles in Advance

Select a delivery date 1-2 days before your plans. To help make sure you get an outfit that fits, a free backup size is included in every order. Don’t forget to complete the look with accessories or a handbag! Your rentals will arrive dry cleaned and ready to wear. If they get to you early, you can still keep them until their original due date. When your rental period is up, return your items using the included prepaid shipping label.
